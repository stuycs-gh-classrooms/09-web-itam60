Fun Facts about Giant Siphonophores

  • They can grow up to 160 ft long!
  • Instead of individual organisms, they are actually colonies of specialized tiny animals called "zooids".
  • Giant siphonophores live about 3000 ft below sea level.
  • They attract prey using bioluminescence, then kill them with stinging nematocysts, like jellyfish!
